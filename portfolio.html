Task 8: Big Data. Extracting a story from millions of prices

I processed the LRPD (40M+ rows) by filtering for specific staple items and collapsing thousands of price quotes into monthly averages. This reveals long-term inflation trends for a supermarket staple vs. a service-based luxury.

Note: To prevent website lag, the data was reduced from millions of observations to monthly means using Python in Google Colab before visualization.
